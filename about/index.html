Our Story

From one home to hundreds — a passion for beautiful windows.

Izhaan Decor Inc was born out of a simple belief: every window in your home deserves to look its best. Starting right here in Mississauga, we set out to bring premium, custom-fitted window coverings to new homeowners who wanted quality without the guesswork.

Today we work across Mississauga delivering zebra blinds, roller blinds, vertical blinds, drapery, and more — all measured, sourced, and installed by our own hands. We take pride in treating every client's home as if it were our own.
