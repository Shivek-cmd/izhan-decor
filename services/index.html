01 — Zebra Blinds

Light control that moves with your mood.

Zebra blinds — also known as day-night blinds — feature alternating sheer and solid fabric bands that layer to give you complete control over privacy and light. A modern favourite for living rooms, kitchens, and bedrooms.

  • Alternating sheer & solid fabric bands
  • Precise light control at any time of day
  • Available in a wide range of colours & textures
  • Child-safe cordless options available
  • Easy to clean, long-lasting materials

02 — Roller Blinds

Clean lines. Minimal design. Maximum function.

Roller blinds are the definition of understated elegance. A single fabric panel rolls smoothly up and down to control light — available in blackout, light-filtering, or solar options to suit any room in your home.

  • Blackout, light-filtering & solar fabric options
  • Clean, seamless look for modern interiors
  • Moisture-resistant options for kitchens & bathrooms
  • Custom sizing for any window width
  • Motorised options available on request

03 — Vertical Blinds

The perfect solution for large windows & patio doors.

Vertical blinds are built for wide openings. Individual fabric or PVC slats hang vertically and rotate to control light and privacy — ideal for sliding patio doors, wide bay windows, and open-plan living areas.

  • Ideal for sliding doors & wide windows
  • Fabric & PVC slat options
  • Full blackout or light-filtering configurations
  • Available in neutral & bold colour palettes
  • Durable hardware built for daily use
Get a Quote

04 — Sheer Blinds

Soft light. Subtle privacy. All day beauty.

Sheer blinds gently diffuse natural light while maintaining your view to the outside. Perfect for living rooms and dining areas where you want a bright, airy feel without sacrificing privacy during the day.

  • Soft diffused natural light all day
  • Daytime privacy without blocking the view
  • Elegant, flowing appearance
  • Available in sheer-to-semi-sheer weights
  • Pairs beautifully with blackout drapes
Get a Quote

05 — California Blinds

Contemporary style. Versatile light control.

California blinds combine the look of a roller blind with the privacy control of a sheer. Wide fabric vanes pivot between open and closed, giving you a sophisticated look with exceptional light management — popular in modern and transitional homes.

  • Wide fabric vanes for contemporary look
  • Pivoting vanes for precise light & privacy control
  • Sheer & room-darkening fabric options
  • Available in a variety of textures & tones
  • Smooth chain or motorised operation

06 — Drapery & Curtains

Tailored fabric that transforms a room.

Custom drapery adds warmth, texture, and sophistication to any space. Whether you prefer floor-length panels for a formal dining room or relaxed linen drapes for a cozy bedroom, we help you choose the perfect fabric, lining, and hanging style.

  • Custom-cut to your exact window height & width
  • Full blackout lining options available
  • Wide fabric selection — linen, velvet, sheer & more
  • Eyelet, pleat & rod-pocket heading styles
  • Professional installation included
